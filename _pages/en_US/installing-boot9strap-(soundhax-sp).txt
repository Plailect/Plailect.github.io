### Compatibility Notes

This Soundhax method (when combined with universal-otherapp) is compatible with the following versions and regions.

* If USA/EUR/JPN region (with a U/E/J in the version string, ie. 11.3.0-##U), the two numbers before the region in the system version string is between 11 and 36.
* If KOR region (with a K in the version string, ie. 11.3.0-##K), the two numbers before the region in the system version string is between 11 and 32.
